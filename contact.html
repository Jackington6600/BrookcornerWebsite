Contact, Bookings & About us About Us We live a few yards from the apartment and have done so since Summer 2000 when Cressbrook Mill was redeveloped. So we can personally recommend the location, walks and surrounding area. It also means we are on hand for key collection or any queries during your stay, without being intrusive. We have run the apartment as a holiday let since 2007, but please note that the internal photos are from 2016. We have many regulars, who visit once or more each year, as they find the apartment to be peaceful, clean and easy to use.
Booking Procedure: Book Direct: You can book direct using the telephone or email contact details below. This is the cheapest way to book. We request a small deposit to secure the booking and then ask for balance 6 weeks prior to arrival. Payments can be made by cheque or BACS.
